About

I am a Graphic Designer and Illustrator with 5 years of experience using Adobe Creative Suite, primarily applications such as Illustrator, Indesign, XD, and Photoshop. I also dabble in web design and front-end development. Other areas of expertise would include fine art such as painting, drawing, and writing.

I encourage you to browse through my portfolio to get a good idea of the type of work that I do and if that interests you. I also offer a variety of design services so please contact me here for inquries.
